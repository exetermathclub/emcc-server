Online Tournament
The EMCC Online Tournament is the online version of the EMCC. You submit your answers through an online interface which will be available here during the EMCC. It is held simultaneously as the EMCC, and the problems are identical. It is also free. Results will be published alongside the on-site results. However, the result of the online competition is more casual than the on-site one. For instance, there will be no prizes awarded to online tournament winners.
