Parking

Visitors to the Academy may park in the semi-circle in front of Jeremiah Smith Hall, in front of and behind Admissions (Bissell House), in any parking lot with assigned visitor parking and on nearby public streets. The closest parking for EMC2 participants is the roadside parking on Front Street, Tan Lane, and other public streets nearby.

Lodging

We have favorable quotes from the Fairfield Inn at Exeter for about $80 a night. We also have vouchers available for the Fairfield Inn at Exeter, and teams coming from a distance of 150 miles or more from Exeter can apply for these vouchers. If you are interested, please tell us how many teams do you plan to bring, and we will do our best to accommodate you. If you are interested, please contact us at emcc2014@gmail.com for details.
